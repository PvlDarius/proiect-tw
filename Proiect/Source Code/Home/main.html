What is "Clinic App"?


  "Clinic App" is a platform made for doctors to manage their patients more easily and keep track of thei medical records, previous and future visits.

  Also here, patients can check their records too and can book appointments to our registered doctors.

  We aim to make communication between doctors and patients much easier in a proffessional way.
